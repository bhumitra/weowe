A drop of water is worth more than a sack of gold to a thirsty man ! Ab har ghar pe pani bahega!

Our mission is to help the needy people who have encountered hardships and are struggling to survive without running water in their homes. We do not need to rely on charities or government. We help people raise funds to pay their water bills !
